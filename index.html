ESPRESSO

C2D2AI NLP System for Neuroimaging Report Processing

About ESPRESSO

ESPRESSO is an advanced Natural Language Processing system designed to process neuroimaging reports and classify patients' Small Vessel Disease Burden Index (SBI) and White Matter Disease (WMD) status. This system provides automated analysis of medical reports to support clinical decision-making and research.
